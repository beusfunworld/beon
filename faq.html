FAQ

Answers to common questions about creating with Paint Online.

Is Paint Online a safe creative environment?

Absolutely. The game is designed to be a safe space for creativity. It contains no ads, no in-app purchases, and no external links, allowing you to focus on developing your art skills.

Can I save my artwork?

Yes! After you have finished a drawing, a 'Save' button allows you to download the image. This is a great way to track your progress and share your achievements.

Can I play on a tablet or phone?

Yes. The game is fully responsive and designed to work perfectly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones. The interface is easy to use, making creativity accessible anywhere.

How do I interact with the game?

The controls are simple: tap a color, then tap the area of the drawing to fill it. This intuitive "paint bucket" style is designed for touch and mouse, helping you focus on color choices rather than complex controls.

Is this game free to use?

Absolutely. Paint Online is completely free. We are committed to providing an accessible and effective art experience for everyone without any cost.
